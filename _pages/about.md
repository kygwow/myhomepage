<section class="home-hero">
  <p class="home-lead">Senior Research Engineer, <a href="https://imaging.snu.ac.kr/team">Imaging Intelligence Laboratory</a> @ Seoul National University</p>
  <p class="home-kicker">Computational imaging / Forward models / Optical metrology</p>
  <p>I develop computational imaging methods around physics-aware forward models: connecting wave-optics simulation, optical system design, and data-driven reconstruction for problems where hardware encoding and computational inference must be designed together.</p>
</section>

<section class="home-section">
  <h2>Current Research</h2>
  <div class="home-area-list">
    <div class="home-area">
      <h3>Forward-model-driven Computational Microscopy</h3>
      <p>Extending Fourier ptychography and reflection-mode computational microscopy beyond scalar approximations toward high-NA, polarization-aware, and metrology-oriented imaging.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="home-area">
      <h3>Vector Diffraction and Wave-Matter Interaction</h3>
      <p>Modeling polarization mixing, angle-dependent reflection, and local wave-matter interaction for optical systems where simplified propagation models become limiting.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="home-area">
      <h3>Differentiable Optics-Mechanics Simulation</h3>
      <p>Building simulation frameworks that couple geometry deformation, wave propagation, and optical encoding for deformable, phase-coded, and adaptive computational imaging systems.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
